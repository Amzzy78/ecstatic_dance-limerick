GUIDELINES

THE DANCE FLOOR IS A SACRED SPACE

BE FREE. DANCE HOW YOU WANT. NO JUDGEMENT. BE PRESENT. NO PHONES. NO CAMERAS. BE CLEAR. NO INTOXICANTS. BE EMBODIED. NO TALKING. BE CONSENSUAL. IF IT’S NOT A CLEAR YES, IT’S A NO.

Entering the space

Ambient music will be playing softly and you may do some light stretching to prepare the body to transitions into a freeform dance journey.
At the end of the set we close with a sound healing meditation to complete the experience.

On the Dance Floor

People of all walks of life, shapes and sizes are welcome to dance however they please. We ask that all honor the shared space and one another.

Main Guidelines

  • No talking on the dance floor
  • Honor the no drugs/substances/alcohol policy
  • Do not take pictures/video of the dance floor or other participants
  • Barefoot unless for medical reasons can wear socks

    • These core guidelines are what keep the ecstatic dance floor an intentional, sacred, and safe space for you to dive deep into the experience.
