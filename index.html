MOVEMENT AS MEDITATION

ECSTATIC DANCE IS A FREE FORM MOVEMENT

Movement as Meditation and Medicine

We invite you to experience a deep connection, release stored emotions, dance, enjoy the flow of music and the community we will build together.

"Energy moves in waves. Waves move in patterns. Patterns move in rhythms. A human being is just that energy, waves, patterns, rhythms. Nothing more. Nothing less. A dance." Gabrielle Roth

Do I need to be “spiritual” or know how to “meditate” to come?

No, just dance for the sheer joy of it.

Do I need dance experience?

No. This is a movement for all people, all bodies.

What should I wear?

Whatever makes you comfortable and free to move.

What kind of music is played?

Various electronic and world music genres.

DANCE IN WHATEVER WAY FEELS RIGHT TO YOU

How would you dance if you thought nobody was looking?
Ecstatic Dance today has become a movement with communities growing all over the world.
Ecstatic Dance is a self-led mind-body practice of uncensored spontaneous movement. It is up to you to create your form.
Explore the possibility of dropping your inhibitions and just letting loose into a playground where total freedom of expression is the norm.
The roots of Ecstatic Dance are ancient. As a species, throughout history, we danced for spiritual, emotional, and community-building experiences.

EVENT

Freeform dance requires you to be present. The Ecstatic Dance floor becomes a sacred space to dive into the present moment.

Conscious and intentional ecstatic dance can help to process emotions that get “stuck” in the body. It helps people to express joy, anxiety, aggression, ecstasy, vulnerability in a safe space.
