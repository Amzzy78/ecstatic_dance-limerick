MOVEMENT MEDICINE

ECSTATIC DANCE IS A FREE FORM MOVEMENT

Movement as Meditation and Medicine

We invite you to experience a deep connection, release stored emotions, dance it out of you, enjoy the flow of music and the community we will build together.

"Energy moves in waves. Waves move in patterns. Patterns move in rhythms. A human being is just that energy, waves, patterns, rhythms. Nothing more. Nothing less. A dance." Gabrielle Roth

Do I need to be “spiritual” or know how to “meditate” to come?

No, just dance for the sheer joy of it.

Do I need dance experience?

No. This is movement for all people, all bodies.

What should I wear?

What ever makes you comfortable and free to move.

What kind of music is played?

Various electronic and world music genres.

DANCE WHAT EVER WAY FEELS RIGHT TO YOU. THIS IS A NO JUDGEMENT SPACE.

Ecstatic Dance is self-led mind body practice of uncensored spontaneous movement. It is up to you to create your own form.

How would you dance if you thought nobody was looking?

Explore the possibility of dropping your inhibitions and just letting loose into a playground where total freedom of expression is the norm.
